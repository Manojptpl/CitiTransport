Logistic Service

The Logistics Division of the FSL Group is a full service transportation provider. While we can be relied upon to manage those singular, specialized shipments, we can also effectively sustain your day-to-day supply chain requirements. In fact, because of the relationships we maintain with hundreds of vendors, we can often provide pricing and solutions that might not otherwise be accessible, especially to smaller shippers. With our highly qualified, hands-on operation staff and our substantial pool of diverse resources, if we don’t already have an answer for you, we know how to find one quickly.

Some distinctive advantages to employing our Logistics services are:

  • Highly personalized customer service and a commitment to go the extra mile to achieve customer satisfaction

  • Determination to ensure that each shipment is executed efficiently – from pickup to delivery

  • Analysis to eliminate recurring service failure in problematic areas

  • Regional and national warehousing and distribution capacities

  • Expedited Service

  • Web-based visibility of current status of shipments from pick up to delivery

  • POD retrieval

  • Customized transportation management reports

  • Problem solving proficiency regarding both transactional and persistent supply chain complexities

  • And FSL Logistics is equipped to provide virtually any mode of transport you require, including:

  • LTL

  • Partials – Van or Flatbed

  • Truckload – Van or Flatbed

  • Specialty Equipment (heavy-hauling, over dimensional, crane delivery, etc.)

  • Rail

  • Ocean – CL or LCL

  • Air/Expedited

  • Customized transportation management reports

  • Problem solving proficiency regarding both transactional and persistent supply chain complexities

We’re happy to provide consultation to identify and address all of our client’s transportation, warehousing, and distribution needs.

With FSL Logistics, you need only make one contact: we’ll take it from there!
